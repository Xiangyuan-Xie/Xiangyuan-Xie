# Hi, I'm Xiangyuan Xie 👋

🚀 I am a first-year Master’s student specializing in **Robot Learning**, **Teleoperation Systems**, and **Mobile Manipulation**. During my internship at the **Beijing Innovation Center of Humanoid Robotics (X-Humanoid)**, I was deeply engaged in the research and development of teleoperation systems. I have strong hands-on experience in real-world robotics deployment and a proven track record in competitive robotics, including securing National 2nd place in RoboCup China and National 3rd place in the Smart Car Competition. I am constantly building bridges between simulation environments and physical hardware, exploring complex control methods for robotic manipulators and mobile platforms.
